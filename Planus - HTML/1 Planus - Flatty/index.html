About Me

Who Am I?

I am a web developer. I like to focus on front-end web development and make websites and web applications look pretty across browsers and platforms. Whether creating or designing, I aim to make the user experience and interaction a memorable piece of art.

You will find me learning, building, and improving - always. When I am not attending meet-ups to further my knowledge, I can be spotted working with others on collaborative projects. I enjoy the proactive social learning process from interacting with fellow developers and designers; the opportunity to make a new friend or gain a cool trick is invaluable.

My Toolbox Inclues:

  • HTML, HTML5, XHTML, CSS, CSS3, Bootstrap, Ionic
  • JavaScript, jQuery, JSON, Ajax, DOM manipulation
  • MEAN stack: MongoDB, Express, Angular.js, Node.js
  • Responsive Web Development (media queries)
  • Wireframing, Prototyping, Balsamiq, OmniGraffle
  • Currently Mastering: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe Photoshop

Where Can You Find Me?

Everywhere! When I am not at the computer, you can find me outdoors practicing yoga, at a hidden hole-in-the-wall establishment indulging in unique dishes, or on random adventures snapping photos for my budding obsession with storytelling photography.
